Welcome to Robotics Club NIT Patna

Established in 2010, the Robotics Club at NIT Patna is a premier student-run organization dedicated to fostering innovation, research, and hands-on learning in the field of robotics and automation. We serve as a platform for students to explore, create, and innovate in robotics.

Our club brings together students from various engineering disciplines who share a common passion for robotics. We believe in learning by doing, and our projects range from simple autonomous robots to complex AI-powered systems.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty mentors, and a vibrant community of robotics enthusiasts, we provide an environment where theoretical knowledge meets practical application.

Club History

A journey of innovation, growth, and milestones since our inception

2010

Club Foundation

Robotics Club NIT Patna was founded by a group of passionate students with guidance from faculty mentors. Started with just 15 members and basic equipment.

2012

First National Competition Win

Won 1st prize at National Robotics Competition with an autonomous line-following robot. This victory put NIT Patna on the national robotics map.

2015

Advanced Robotics Lab

Established a dedicated robotics laboratory with 3D printers, CNC machines, and advanced testing equipment through institute funding and industry partnerships.

2018

International Collaboration

Partnered with robotics clubs from Singapore and Germany for joint research projects. Published first international research paper on swarm robotics.

2021

AI & ML Integration

Incorporated Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning into robotics projects. Developed first AI-powered autonomous drone for precision agriculture.

2024

Current & Future Vision

Over 200 active members, 50+ ongoing projects, and partnerships with leading tech companies. Focusing on sustainable robotics and human-robot collaboration for societal impact.
