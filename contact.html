Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the Robotics Club

How can I join the Robotics Club?

We conduct recruitment at the beginning of each academic year. Keep an eye on campus announcements or visit our lab during orientation week. No prior experience required - we welcome enthusiasts from all branches.

Do I need prior robotics experience?

Not at all! We have programs for complete beginners. Our workshops start from basics and progress to advanced topics. The only requirement is enthusiasm and willingness to learn.

What equipment is available for use?

Our lab is equipped with 3D printers, Arduino/Raspberry Pi kits, sensors, motors, drones, and testing equipment. Members receive training before using specialized equipment.

Are there any fees for joining?

No, membership is completely free for NIT Patna students. The club is funded by the institute and through industry collaborations. Some advanced workshops may have minimal material costs.

Can non-members attend workshops?

Yes, many of our workshops are open to all NIT Patna students. However, priority is given to club members for hands-on sessions due to limited equipment availability.

How often does the club meet?

We have regular meetings every Friday at 4 PM in the Robotics Lab. Additionally, project teams meet as needed, and workshops are scheduled throughout the semester.
